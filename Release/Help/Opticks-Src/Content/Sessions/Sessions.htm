Sessions

A session is a period of work or activity within Opticks. By using the session options in Opticks, you can save the work you're doing rather than having to recreate it. Saving a session saves all your work as well as the placement of all Opticks windows at the time that the session is saved.

Sessions are a powerful tool for saving and managing complex analysis work within Opticks.

The Session Explorer is a window that enables you to manage your Opticks session and provides access to some of Opticks' related functionality.

Note: By default, Opticks automatically saves the current session every 15 minutes. You can change this interval in your session options.
