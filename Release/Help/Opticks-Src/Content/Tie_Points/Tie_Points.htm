Tie Points

A tie point indicates a feature common to two scenes such as a building, a road, an intersection, or a unique geographical feature. The tie points functionality in Opticks enables you to load two scenes into Opticks and view the tie points created by a tie points algorithm.

Use the Opticks zoom functionality to see the pixel values of tie points.
