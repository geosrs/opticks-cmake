GeoTIFF View Exporter Options

The GeoTIFF View Exporter allows you to export a two-dimensional image of the view window. For product windows, this means that the paper and any background are saved in the image.

  1. In the Image Size, enter the Width and Height of the exported image.
  2. To lock the aspect ratio, click the Lock Aspect Ratio button. This button is a toggle.
  3. Under Compression Options, in the Rows Per Strip field, enter the number of rows per strip.
  4. Check the Pack Bits checkbox to turn the compression on.
  5. Click the OK button.
